## 1. Executive Summary

LiNKsites is a template-first website factory that sells bundled outcomes: pre-built websites, managed hosting, and standardized operating services.

The business line is designed to generate fast cash flow with controlled delivery risk by reusing shared infrastructure, shared templates, and shared operating workflows.

## 2. Business Definition

LiNKsites addresses SMBs that need a professional web presence without custom-agency timelines or costs.

Primary customer groups:

- service SMBs with weak websites and poor conversion paths,
- owner-operated businesses that need managed execution, not tools alone,
- businesses that can start with a site and later buy automations and bot services.

Primary offers:

- Standard: lightweight shared-runtime website package,
- Premium: richer shared-runtime package with blog and automation allowances,
- Enterprise: dedicated-runtime package with deeper operating support.

## 4. Value Proposition

LiNKsites wins by combining speed, reuse, and managed operations:

- pre-built delivery from proven templates,
- lower cost from shared operations and shared infrastructure,
- consistent quality through QA checklists and governed workflows,
- clear upsell path into LiNKautowork and LiNKbots.

## 5. Market and Customer Strategy

Launch and expansion rule:

- start with one active locale (United States or Japan),
- open second locale when the first reaches 20 active LiNKsites customers.

Customer selection criteria:

- clear business problem with measurable value from a new site,
- fit with template-first delivery and bounded customization,
- viable recurring relationship with low long-term support burden.

## 6. Delivery and Operating Model

Core workflow:

1. LiNKbot research and lead qualification by niche and location.
2. Lead insertion into CRM with qualification metadata.
3. Template adaptation to produce a finished pre-sale asset.
4. Internal QA and packaging by Standard, Premium, or Enterprise scope.
5. Outreach and closing by human sales and/or LiNKbot-assisted follow-up.
6. Go-live, domain mapping, managed operations, and upsell lifecycle.

LiNKbot role in LiNKsites:

- site factory orchestration and checklist execution,
- lead generation and CRM enrichment,
- follow-up task automation for human closers,
- post-sale operational routing to support and automation teams.

## 7. Technology and Infrastructure

Core architecture:

- control-plane droplet for CMS and core operations,
- shared runtime droplets for Standard and Premium clients,
- dedicated runtime isolation for Enterprise clients,
- central Payload CMS and Supabase-backed data services.

LiNKsites management allocation baseline:

- DigitalOcean base allocation: $7.92/month,
- weekly backup allocation: $4.80/month,
- shared Supabase allocation: $6.25/month,
- total management layer allocation: $18.97/month.

## 8. Business Model and Pricing

Standard package:

- setup price: $300,
- recurring price: $14.99/month (1-year) or $11.99/month effective (2-year),
- internal recurring cost: $1.72/site/month,
- recurring gross margin: 88.53% (1-year), 85.65% (2-year),
- estimated token build cost: about $0.45/site.

Premium package:

- setup price: $750,
- recurring price: $29.99/month (1-year) or $24.99/month effective (2-year),
- internal recurring cost: $4.10/site/month,
- recurring gross margin: 86.33% (1-year), 83.59% (2-year),
- estimated token build cost: about $1.85/site.

Enterprise package:

- setup price: $1,999,
- recurring price: $249.99/month (1-year) or $199.99/month effective (2-year),
- internal recurring cost: about $37.30/site/month,
- recurring gross margin: 85.08% (1-year), 81.35% (2-year),
- enterprise cost includes average domain allowance of about $15/year,
- estimated token build cost: about $4.90/site.

Overage model:

- outbound transfer: $0.013 per GiB,
- DB egress: $0.117 per GB,
- DB size: $0.1625 per GB-month,
- DB storage: $0.0273 per GB-month,
- DB MAU where applicable: $0.004225 per MAU-month,
- Premium service overages: automations $0.010 per execution, AI responses $0.008 per response, extra blog posts $24 each,
- Enterprise service overages: automations $0.008 per execution, AI responses $0.006 per response, extra blog posts $20 each.

## 9. Dependencies and Shared Services

- LiNKskills for governed logic and reusable operational skills,
- LiNKautowork for automation execution and support workflows,
- LiNKbots for orchestration, lead ops, and workflow execution,
- QA and admin for quality, legal, finance, and reporting controls,
- LiNKtrend Media for demand generation and content support.

## 10. 12-Month Objectives and KPIs

Year-one objectives:

- close first 4 to 6 paying clients within 90 days,
- reach 20 plus active sites in first locale before opening second locale,
- maintain package discipline and controlled support burden,
- increase upsell rate into LiNKautowork and high-value recurring services.

Core KPIs:

- prebuilt sites generated per week,
- outreach-to-close ratio,
- setup fee cash collected,
- package mix (Standard/Premium/Enterprise),
- recurring margin by package,
- churn and retention by package,
- overage recovery rate versus provider overage cost.

## 11. Management Recommendation

LiNKsites should be managed as a productized operating system, not as custom project work. The business should enforce package boundaries, keep delivery template-driven, use LiNKbots for factory and CRM throughput, and scale geography only when customer density and operating quality justify expansion.
